✦ KEY STORIES ✦

Recovering a Trading Platform After a Database Disaster

Nomura Star Award
[ The Situation ]

A critical data center human error accidentally wiped out a production database along with its live backups, erasing thousands of active FX trades. While downstream systems already held records of these transactions, our source trading engine was blank, facing an impending Monday morning market opening.

[ The Engineering ]

I spent the weekend isolating the precise pool of missing trades by harvesting raw message payloads retained in our trading infrastructure. I engineered a standalone Java recovery utility to re-inject, parse, and safely auto-book every single trade sequentially while managing downstream cancellation feeds.

[ The Impact ]

Before the opening bell commenced, the system context was restored completely with absolute trade accuracy and zero operational disruption to the business. Honored with a Nomura Star Award for rapid emergency recovery.

The $48M Cross-Jurisdiction Trade Migration Engine

Nomura Star Award
[ The Situation ]

A strict regulatory and strategic window demanded a massive migration of active FX trade positions from western entities into the Tokyo ledger. Our core trading engine represents trade states in highly complex tree structures, making manual cross-entity adjustments an operational nightmare prone to immense noise.

[ The Engineering ]

Collaborating closely with senior Business Analysts to map technical boundary constraints, I worked as the sole engineer to unpack this complex tree layout. I developed a targeted Java tool capable of resolving complex engine anomalies, near-settled states, block trades, and automated position amendments.

[ The Impact ]

Users smoothly uploaded and processed trade books on time with zero post-migration inconsistencies. The successful delivery allowed Nomura to cleanly capture $48M USD in strategic financial and compliance value.

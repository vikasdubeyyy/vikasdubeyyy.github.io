# Beyond Work

## Platelet Donation

I regularly participate in platelet donation initiatives that support cancer patients and oncology treatments.

It is one of the few activities where a few hours of your time can directly help someone during a difficult phase of life.

Over the years, it has become a meaningful personal commitment.

---

## Trekking

I enjoy trekking and exploring mountain trails whenever time permits.

Long climbs, changing conditions, and extended journeys require preparation, patience, and persistence.

---

## Motorcycle Touring

Motorcycle touring is a relatively new hobby that I have started exploring on my TVS Ronin.

I enjoy discovering new routes, planning journeys, and experiencing places that are often missed when travelling quickly.

For me, it is a good reminder that progress is often more enjoyable when the journey itself is appreciated.
